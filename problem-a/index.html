Gandalf the Grey

Gandalf the Grey is a fictional character from the world of Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit, books written by J.R.R Tolkien. In the stories, Gandalf is a wizard or istari who helps the main characters defeat Sauron.

I admire Gandalf for the following reasons:

  • He is selfless in his deeds, helping the hobbits while putting himself at risk
  • He is the only of the five istari who remained uncorrupted
  • He is able to find time to relax: launching fireworks and smoking his pipe
